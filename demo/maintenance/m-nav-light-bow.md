The bicolor bow light flickers when the pulpit is loaded — leaning on it or
clipping a jackline to it makes the light cut in and out. That points to wiring
chafe at the pulpit base where the run passes through the deck and flexes with
the rail.

Replacing the fixture with a sealed LED bicolor unit (lower draw, no bulb to
fail) and renewing the chafed length of wire with a proper grommet through the
deck. Cheap part, but a nav light you can't rely on at night is not optional.

## Steps
- [x] Reproduce flicker by loading pulpit.
- [ ] Open fixture, inspect terminals.
- [ ] Fit sealed LED bicolor.
- [ ] Renew chafed wire run.
