Noticed on the club day-sail: the automatic bilge pump float switch cycles
erratically — short-cycling with almost no water in the bilge, then sometimes
not triggering at all. The pump itself runs fine when switched to manual, so the
fault is in the float switch, not the pump.

Replacing it with a wide-angle float switch, which is far less prone to nuisance
cycling than the small panel-mount type. While the panel is open, check the
harness terminals for corrosion and heat-shrink anything suspect.

## Steps
- [x] Confirm pump OK on manual.
- [ ] Buy wide-angle float switch.
- [ ] Replace switch + heat-shrink terminals.
- [ ] Test auto cycle.
