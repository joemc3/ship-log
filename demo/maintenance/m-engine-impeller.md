The engine overheated under sustained power on the Port Almira delivery, the
alarm sounding after about an hour at cruising rpm. Backed off the throttle and
sailed the rest of the leg. Back in the slip I pulled the raw-water pump cover
and found the impeller missing two vanes.

Do NOT run the engine again until the broken vanes are recovered from the heat
exchanger downstream of the pump — loose rubber there will block tubes and cause
the same overheat (or worse) even with a fresh impeller fitted.

## Steps
- [x] Confirm weak raw-water flow.
- [x] Pull pump cover, inspect impeller.
- [ ] Fit new impeller + cover gasket.
- [ ] Recover lost vanes from heat exchanger.
- [ ] Run up and verify temp + flow.
