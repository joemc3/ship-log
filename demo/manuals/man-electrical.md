Boat-specific 12 V DC reference for *Valkyrie*. Covers the two battery banks, the
main panel, and the two circuits that most often need attention underway.

## Battery banks {#banks}

Two banks:

- **House:** a single Group 27 AGM under the starboard settee
- **Start:** a dedicated start battery under the port settee

A **1/2/Both** switch at the nav station selects the source. Normal underway:
House (1) for service loads, Start (2) reserved for cranking; use Both only to
parallel for a hard start, then return to 1.

## Panel + circuits {#panel}

Main panel breakers, port to starboard:

- Nav lights
- VHF
- Bilge pump (auto / manual)
- Cabin lights
- Instruments

## Nav lights {#navlights}

The bow carries a **bicolor (red/green) light**; stern and steaming/anchor lights
are separate breakers. The bow fixture is on the open list — see
m-nav-light-bow for the weak/intermittent bicolor.

## Bilge pump {#bilge}

The bilge pump is wired through the panel for **manual** run and through an
**auto float switch** for unattended operation, so it runs with the panel off.
The float switch is currently intermittent — see m-bilge-pump-switch.
