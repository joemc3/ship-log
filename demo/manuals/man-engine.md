Reference manual for the Universal M-25, the 21 hp freshwater-cooled marine
diesel fitted to *Valkyrie*. Keep a printed copy at the nav station; this file
is the quick index.

## Winterizing {#winterize}

Drain the raw-water side, run RV/propylene-glycol antifreeze through the cooling
loop until it runs pink at the exhaust, and fog the cylinders before lay-up.
Close the engine raw-water seacock and tag the key.

## Fuel system {#fuel}

The primary fuel filter takes **Racor 2-micron** elements — carry two spares.
Bleed the system at the secondary filter and the injection pump after any filter
change or run-dry. Keep the tank topped over winter to limit condensation.

## Raw-water cooling {#cooling}

The raw-water pump uses a **Sherwood 10615K** impeller. Inspect each spring and
after any overheating event; a shredded impeller can lodge vanes in the heat
exchanger. See the open impeller job (m-engine-impeller) for the current change
that came out of the Port Almira passage overheat.

## Belt + alternator {#belt}

One V-belt drives both the raw-water pump and the alternator. Tension for about
1/2 in deflection at the midspan; check for glazing and black dust at every oil
change.

## Specifications {#specs}

21 hp at 3000 rpm, 3-cylinder, freshwater-cooled with heat exchanger. Oil:
15W-40 diesel-rated, ~3 qt with filter. Idle ~800 rpm.
